# 🚢 Oh Ship!

> **A fast-paced 2-player competitive party game set aboard a cruise ship — developed in Unity (C#) in 1 month as part of a university course project.**

---

## 📖 Overview

**Oh Ship!** is a local 2-player competitive game inspired by titles like *Overcooked!* and *Moving Out*. Both players race against the clock on the same ship, completing minigames to score as many points as possible before time runs out. The one with the highest score wins.

The game was built in approximately **one month** by a small team as part of the *Projectes 3* course at **ENTI-UB (Escola de Noves Tecnologies Interactives, Universitat de Barcelona)**.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

Two players compete simultaneously on a shared ship deck. Each player must complete on-board minigames independently — fishing, mopping, or sorting the catch into the correct pile — while managing hazards and environmental obstacles that pop up during the round.

The session is timed. When the clock hits zero, the player with the most points wins.

### Minigames & Systems
- **Fishing** — Interact with fishing spots to catch items and carry them to the correct pile
- **Mopping** — Clean up spills and hazards scattered across the deck
- **Sorting** — Deliver caught fish to their matching scoring zone
- **Hazards / Handicaps** — Dynamic events that disrupt both players and force quick adaptation
- **Score & Timer HUD** — Live per-player score display with a countdown timer that changes color as time runs out
